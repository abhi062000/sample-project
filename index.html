Find Your Lockdown Style

While many of us have (or have not! ) a personal style - that is, a fashion 'aesthetic' that describes how you dress - the coronavirus may have thrown it off. Usually, we are out and working, but during the lockdown, many of us are at home, with the motto 'Netflix and chill', or the rather more current one, 'quarantine and chill'. What we look like matters, especially in these special circumstances. We behave better and exhibit more manners when we are dressed properly, instead of when we look like a slob. Changing out of sleepwear does help work performance, and you'll get less energy slumps (pajamas send the subliminal message to the brain that it's nighttime). So, what's your lockdown style?.
